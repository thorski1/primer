Features

Designed for young learners

✨
Puck, Your Companion
Puck guides every lesson with warmth and encouragement. Wrong answers are just steps toward the right one.
🎯
Placement Quiz
Puck asks a few questions at the start to find the best chapter to begin — not too easy, not too hard.
📅
Daily Challenge
One special challenge each day with bonus XP. Short sessions that build a habit of learning.
⭐
Star Ratings
Zones earn 1-3 stars. Revisit any zone to earn a perfect score and see how much you've grown.
🔖
Bookmarks
Save any challenge to revisit later with [b]. Great for things worth remembering.
🏅
Completion Certificate
Finish the full campaign and receive a beautiful ASCII completion certificate to print and keep.
💾
Auto-Save
Progress saves automatically between every session. Pick up exactly where you left off.
🔄
Auto-Updates
New chapters and challenges delivered automatically. The Primer grows with your child.

A note for parents

⏱️
Short, focused sessions
Each zone takes 5–15 minutes. The Primer is designed for daily short sessions, not long marathons. Perfect before or after school.
👨‍👧
Read it together
The Primer works as a solo adventure or as a shared experience — read the lessons together, discuss the questions, celebrate every right answer.
🌱
Age-appropriate throughout
Every challenge is carefully designed for its age range. The Primer celebrates curiosity, kindness, and growth — no frustration, only encouragement.
📖
Inspired by The Diamond Age
Inspired by the Primer from Neal Stephenson's novel — an AI companion that grows with the child and teaches everything she needs to become who she's meant to be.
